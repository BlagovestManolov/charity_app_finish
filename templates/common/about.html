How we started.

Two years ago, a non-governmental project called "Childhood Love" began to unfold in Sofia, which aimed to help children without parents or at social risk. This was the story of a young woman who started an exciting enterprise - Elena.

Elena was a social worker with a strong desire to help children who were experiencing difficulties in their lives. There were moments in her past that affected her in a special way, creating in her an understanding and compassion for those who needed love and support.

Determined to make a difference in the lives of these children, Elena gathered a group of committed and willing volunteers with similar views and hearts full of desire to help. Together they became a team that set out to create a center for orphaned children where they could receive the support, education and love they needed.

With the help of donors, local companies and public foundations, "Childhood Love" was able to open its doors to children who are abandoned or live in difficult family conditions. In the center they found love and care, they wanted to express themselves through creativity and sports, to receive education and support to have a chance for a better future.
